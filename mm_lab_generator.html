M&M Lab Random Distribution Generator

Refresh the page or click Generate New Results to get a new virtual bag and two new sampling experiments.

What this page does

This page creates one virtual set of 40 M&Ms, records the color counts, then simulates:

  1. 24 ordered pairs with replacement
  2. 24 ordered pairs without replacement within each pair (both candies returned before the next pair)

Default color mix used for the virtual bag: Blue 24%, Orange 20%, Green 16%, Yellow 14%, Red 13%, Brown 13%.

Population: 40 M&Ms

24 Ordered Pairs: Without Replacement

Within a pair, the first candy is not replaced before the second draw. After the pair is recorded, both candies are returned.
